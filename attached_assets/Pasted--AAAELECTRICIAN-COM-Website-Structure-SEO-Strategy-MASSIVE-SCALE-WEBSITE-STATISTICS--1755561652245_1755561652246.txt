- **Homepage**: "Electrician [City]", "Electrical Services [City]", "Licensed Electrician Near Me"
- **Residential**: "[Service] [City]", "Residential Electrician [City]", "[Service] Near Me"
- **Commercial**: "Commercial Electrician [City]", "[Service] [City]", "Business Electrical [City]"

### GEO Targeting Elements
1. **City/State in Title Tags**: "[Service] in [City], [State] | AAA Electrician"
2. **Local Schema Markup**: LocalBusiness, Service, Review schemas
3. **NAP Consistency**: Name, Address, Phone across all pages
4. **Local Content**: City-specific electrical codes, permits, regulations
5. **Service Area Pages**: Individual pages for each service area/city
6. **Google My Business Integration**: Reviews, photos, business hours

### AIO (AI Optimization) Strategy
1. **Featured Snippet Optimization**: FAQ sections, step-by-step guides
2. **Voice Search Optimization**: Natural language, question-based content
3. **Entity-Based SEO**: Structured data for electrical terms, services
4. **Topic Clusters**: Related services grouped with internal linking
5. **Answer Engine Optimization**: Direct answers to common electrical questions

## Page Content Structure Template

Each page should include:

### Header Section
- **H1**: Primary keyword + location
- **Breadcrumb Navigation**: Home > Service Category > Specific Service
- **Trust Signals**: Licensed, Insured, Years in Business
- **CTA Button**: "Get Free Quote" or "Call Now"

### Content Sections
1. **Service Overview** (150-200 words)
2. **Benefits/Why Choose Us** (200-250 words)
3. **Service Process** (250-300 words)
4. **FAQ Section** (300-400 words)
5. **Service Areas** (100-150 words)
6. **Related Services** (100 words)

### AdSense Placement Areas
- **Header Banner**: 728x90 leaderboard
- **Sidebar**: 300x250 medium rectangle (2-3 units)
- **In-Content**: 336x280 large rectangle (after 2nd paragraph)
- **Footer**: 728x90 leaderboard
- **Mobile**: 320x50 mobile banner, 300x250 mobile rectangle

### Technical SEO Elements

#### Meta Tags Template
```html
<title>[Service] in [City], [State] | AAA Electrician - Licensed & Insured</title>
<meta name="description" content="Professional [service] in [city]. Licensed electrician with 24/7 emergency service. Free estimates, same-day service available. Call (XXX) XXX-XXXX">
<meta name="keywords" content="[service], electrician [city], electrical [service] [city], licensed electrician near me">
```

#### Schema Markup Requirements
1. **LocalBusiness Schema** (Every page)
2. **Service Schema** (Service pages)
3. **Review Schema** (Review aggregation)
4. **FAQ Schema** (FAQ sections)
5. **BreadcrumbList Schema** (Navigation)

#### Page Speed Optimization
- WebP images with alt text including keywords
- Lazy loading for below-fold content
- Minified CSS/JS
- CDN implementation
- Mobile-first responsive design
